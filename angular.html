AngularJS Meetup Berlin

The AngularJS Meetup Berlin is a meetup around the AngularJS framework. We're open to anyone interested in the topic. Whether you're a beginner dying to poke questions at someone with experience or a veteran wanting to discuss the finer details, come along. We can help with specific questions for your problem, write or review some code together.

Join us on Wednesday January 8th 2014 around 7:30pm

Please note that I created a Meetup.com group. If you're interested in our meetup sign up. If you plan on actually attending, please RSVP, to help me planning. I will also use the group to keep you updated should there be any changes and for planning talks.
